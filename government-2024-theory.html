Question: Highlight five functions of the Judiciary in a modern state.

To get full marks, candidates must list and explain:

  • 1. Interpretation of Laws: The primary role is to explain the meaning of the constitution and laws passed by the legislature.
  • 2. Settlement of Disputes: Resolving conflicts between individuals, groups, or the government.
  • 3. Protection of Fundamental Rights: Acting as the "last hope of the common man" by checking executive excesses.

[Marking: 2 marks for each well-explained point up to 10 marks]
