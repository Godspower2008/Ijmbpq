QUESTION 1a
State the principle of conservation of linear momentum and derive the expression for the force exerted by a jet of water striking a vertical wall.
[2 Marks] Definition: The total linear momentum of an isolated system of interacting particles remains constant in both magnitude and direction, provided no external force acts on it.
[3 Marks] Derivation: Let $m$ be the mass of water striking the wall in time $t$. $$F = \frac{\Delta p}{\Delta t} = \frac{m(v - u)}{t}$$ If the water does not rebound ($v = 0$): $$F = \rho A v^2$$ Where $\rho$ is density and $A$ is cross-sectional area.
