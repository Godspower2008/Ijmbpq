Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access the CBT Simulator?
You can access the CBT Simulator by clicking on the "Test" tab in the navigation bar. From there, select your preferred exam mode to start practicing.
Are the past questions up to date?
Yes, we regularly update our database with the latest past questions from IJMB, JAMB, and WAEC bodies to ensure you have the most relevant materials.
Can I use the app offline?
Pro users can download lesson notes and question banks for offline use. Free users require an active internet connection to access the resources.
