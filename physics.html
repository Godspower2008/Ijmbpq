QUESTION 01

A car accelerates uniformly from rest at a rate of $2.0 m/s^2$ for 10 seconds. Calculate the total distance covered by the car during this period.

A
50 Meters
B
100 Meters
C
200 Meters
D
400 Meters

Official Marking Scheme

Using the second equation of motion: $s = ut + \frac{1}{2}at^2$

u = 0 (Rest) [1 Mark]
a = 2.0 m/s² [1 Mark]
t = 10s [1 Mark]

s = 0(10) + 0.5(2.0)(10²)
s = 100 Meters [2 Marks]
