Question 01

Calculate the refractive index of a medium if the angle of incidence is 45° and angle of refraction is 30°.

A. 1.21
B. 1.41
C. 1.50
D. 1.73

Marking Scheme Solution

Using Snell's Law: $n = \frac{\sin(i)}{\sin(r)}$

$n = \frac{\sin(45^\circ)}{\sin(30^\circ)} = \frac{0.7071}{0.5}$

$n = 1.41$
